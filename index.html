About Us

We can help build your project!

We are one of the leading building contractors specializing in construction within the vibrant city of Chandigarh. Our firm, “Gahlot & Sons,” established in 1976 as a Partnership Firm, has garnered a distinguished reputation in the construction industry. Over the years, we have successfully delivered projects spanning Educational, Healthcare, Industrial, Residential, and Commercial domains, catering to both Government and Private clients. With a rich professional history, we have earned accolades from business owners and are acknowledged as leaders due to our proficiency in traditional construction methods and our innovative, forward-thinking approach. The cornerstone of our success lies in our unwavering commitment to quality, impeccable workmanship, and strict adherence to project timelines.

Our Services

Construction Services

We provide construction services that integrate schedule and cost control into day-to-day activities to keep projects on schedule and on budget.

General Contracting

General contracting, often referred to as a traditional delivery system, allows the owner to engage with the architect/engineer to prepare the design of the entire project.

Preconstruction Services

Our preconstruction services ensure maximum return on investment and include planning and coordination, procurement, estimating, scheduling, etc.

Construction Management

We provide construction management services in two phases: preconstruction and construction.
